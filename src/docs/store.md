# Chrome Web Store — PromptBox

## Name
Save ChatGPT Prompts

## Subtitle
Save, organize and reuse your ChatGPT prompts instantly

## Short Description
Quickly save and reuse your best ChatGPT prompts. Stop losing useful prompts and boost your productivity.

## Full Description
Save ChatGPT Prompts is a simple and powerful Chrome extension that helps you store, organize, and reuse your best prompts in seconds.

Stop wasting time searching through chats, notes, and documents. Save your most effective prompts and access them instantly whenever you need them.

Key features:
- Save unlimited prompts
- Organize prompts by categories
- Instant search
- One-click copy to clipboard
- Draft autosave (never lose your work)

Perfect for:
- marketers
- content creators
- developers
- entrepreneurs
- anyone using ChatGPT daily

Boost your productivity and get better results from AI faster.
