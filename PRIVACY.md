# Privacy Policy for PromptBox

**Last updated:** April 29, 2026

## Introduction

PromptBox is a Chrome extension designed to help users save, organize, and reuse text prompts. We are committed to protecting your privacy and keeping your data under your control.

This Privacy Policy explains what information PromptBox stores, how it is used, and what choices you have regarding your data.

## What data is stored

PromptBox stores only the content you choose to save in the extension, such as:

- Prompt text you create or edit
- Related prompt metadata needed for extension functionality (for example, identifiers and timestamps)
- Temporary draft text used for autosave features

PromptBox does **not** collect personal profile information, account credentials, payment information, or browsing history.

## How data is used

Your stored data is used only to provide core extension features, including:

- Saving prompts
- Editing and deleting prompts
- Searching prompts
- Copying prompts to your clipboard
- Restoring draft text in the popup interface

PromptBox does not use your data for advertising, profiling, or marketing.

## Storage location

All PromptBox data is stored locally in your browser using `chrome.storage.local`.

PromptBox does not send your prompt data to external servers controlled by PromptBox.

## Data sharing

PromptBox does not sell, share, rent, or transfer your data to third parties.

PromptBox does not include analytics or tracking services.

## Data security

PromptBox is designed to minimize data exposure by keeping data local to your browser storage.

While no software can guarantee absolute security, PromptBox follows a privacy-by-design approach by avoiding unnecessary data collection and avoiding remote data transmission.

## User control and deletion

You are in control of your data at all times:

- You can delete individual prompts from within the extension
- You can clear all extension data by removing your saved prompts
- You can remove all PromptBox data by uninstalling the extension from Chrome

Because PromptBox does not maintain external servers or user accounts, there is no remote copy of your prompts managed by PromptBox.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or PromptBox privacy practices, please contact the PromptBox project maintainer through the repository where you obtained this extension.
